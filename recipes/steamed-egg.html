Chinese Steamed Eggs

What are Chinese Steamed Eggs?

Chinese steame eggs are a traditional Chinese dish eaten primarily for breakfast. It is an incredibly simple dish for msot of everyone!

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 2/3 volume of eggs in Chicken Stock
  • 1 tbs chili crips
  • 1 tbs soy sauce
  • 1 tbs sesame oil
  • 1 tbs green onion

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into a small bowl and whisk together
  2. Add the Chicken Stock and soy sauce
  3. whisk to combine
  4. Boil a pot of water with about 1 inch of water and a steamer rack
  5. place the bowl into the pot on top of the steamer rack, and drop the temperature to low
  6. Keep heat on until the mixture barely jiggles when the bowl is lighty shaken
  7. Remove from pot
  8. Use a butter knife to cut a cross-hatching pattern into the eggs
  9. Pour Sesame oil on top of mixure and sprinkle green onions on
  10. Enjoy!
